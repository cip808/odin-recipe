Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe

Description

The satisfying crunch on the outside, along with the ooey-goey center will ensure these are the most delicious chocolate chip cookies you've ever tasted!

Ingredients

  1. 8 tablespoons of salted butter
  2. 1/2 cup white sugar
  3. 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla
  5. 1 egg
  6. 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  7. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  8. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  9. 3/4 cup chocolate chips
